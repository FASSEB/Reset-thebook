About Sebastiano Fassina

My journey into health and wellness began with a life-changing diagnosis of Type 1 Diabetes at age 21. Driven by a deep desire for healing, I immersed himself in studying nutrition, neuroscience, and ancient healing methods. Living and working across Italy, Australia, and Canada, I crafted a holistic health approach focused on rhythm, resilience, and the body's natural intelligence. My book, RESET: Restore Your Natural Energy from Within, invites readers to reconnect with their innate energy and rediscover vibrant health in a modern world. Through my experiences, I aim to inspire and support others on their wellness journeys.
